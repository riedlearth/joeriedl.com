Joe Riedl

Award winning journalism Environment Geography Land Climate Home

Joe Riedl is a journalist and photographer whose work centers on news and narrative nonfiction, told through photo-driven stories across a range of mediums. He holds an MSc in geography and a background in climate science, and has worked with Peace Corps, AmeriCorps, the United Nations, and CIFOR across several continents. His reporting has appeared in Willamette Week, High Country News, The Vanguard, and The Pacific Sentinel, among other publications.
